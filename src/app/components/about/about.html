About SideQuest

Connecting communities with skilled tradespeople — local, reliable, and just a click away.

Our Mission

SideQuest was built to solve a simple problem — finding a trustworthy tradesperson nearby shouldn't be hard. Whether you need a plumber, electrician, or carpenter, we make it easy to discover skilled professionals in your area.

We believe in supporting local workers and giving customers the confidence to find help quickly, safely, and affordably.

🔧

Built for Communities

Empowering local tradespeople while giving customers convenient, proximity-based service discovery.

How It Works

🔍

1. Browse Services

Search for the type of service you need and discover providers near you.

📋

2. Request a Provider

Send a request to a provider that fits your needs and wait for confirmation.

✅

3. Get It Done

Your provider shows up and gets the job done. Simple, fast, and reliable.
